Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between AI and Machine Learning?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad field focused on creating systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. Machine Learning (ML) is a subfield of AI that uses algorithms to enable computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. All machine learning is a type of AI, but not all AI is machine learning.

How can AI benefit my business?

AI can significantly boost business performance by automating repetitive tasks, improving decision-making through data analysis, personalizing customer experiences, and enhancing security. It can lead to cost savings, increased efficiency, and a competitive advantage in your industry.

What kind of data do you need for an AI project?

The type of data required depends on the project. For a predictive model, we need historical data related to the outcome you want to predict. For a computer vision project, we need labeled images or videos. The most crucial factor is data quality and relevance, which we help you assess and prepare.

Is AI development an ongoing process?

Yes, AI models need continuous monitoring and maintenance. As data patterns change over time (a phenomenon known as "model drift"), the model's performance can degrade. We offer MLOps services to ensure your AI solution stays accurate and effective through automated monitoring and retraining pipelines.
