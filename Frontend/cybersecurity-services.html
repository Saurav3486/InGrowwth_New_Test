Frequently Asked Questions

What is the first step in a cybersecurity engagement?

The process begins with a comprehensive assessment of your existing security posture. We work with your team to identify critical assets, analyze potential threats, and pinpoint vulnerabilities to create a clear understanding of your current risk level.

How do you help with regulatory compliance?

We provide advisory services to ensure your systems and processes comply with industry-specific regulations and frameworks like GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 27001. We help you implement the necessary controls and provide documentation to prepare you for audits.

Do you offer services for businesses that have already been breached?

Absolutely. Our incident response team provides a rapid response to security breaches. We help you contain the attack, perform a forensic analysis to understand what happened, and develop a recovery plan to get your business back on track while preventing future incidents.

Is employee training really that important for cybersecurity?

Yes, human error is one of the leading causes of cyberattacks, often through phishing and social engineering. We provide tailored training programs to educate your employees on best practices, transforming them from a potential vulnerability into your strongest defense.
