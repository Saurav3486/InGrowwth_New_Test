Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between native and cross-platform mobile apps?

Native apps are built for a specific operating system (iOS or Android) using its native language, offering the best performance and access to all device features. Cross-platform apps are built with a single codebase that can run on both iOS and Android, which is more cost-effective and faster to develop but may have some limitations in performance or access to unique device features.

How long does it take to build a mobile app?

The timeline depends on the app's complexity, features, and platform. A simple app might take 3-6 months, while a complex app with multiple integrations and advanced features could take 9-12 months or longer. We provide a detailed project timeline after the initial discovery phase.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to ensure your mobile app remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. This includes bug fixes, security updates, and adding new features as needed.

What is the typical cost of developing a mobile application?

The cost varies significantly based on factors like the app's complexity, features, design, and chosen platform (native vs. cross-platform). After our initial consultation and discovery, we can provide you with a detailed and transparent quote.
